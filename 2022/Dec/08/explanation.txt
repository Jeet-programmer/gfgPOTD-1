How to Approach:  

How to track the count of the number having 3 divisors.
Clearly, every number greater than 1 have at least 2 divisors 1 and itself.
Now for the incorporating the only 3rd divisor such that total 3 divisors will be there.
This is possible only when the third divisor will be prime.
Finally track down all such number for each query.
